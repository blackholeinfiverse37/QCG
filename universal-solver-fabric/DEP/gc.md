# GC: Governance & Compliance

This document affirms the Governance & Compliance boundaries of the Universal Solver Fabric.

## 1. Compliance Statement
The Universal Solver Fabric is strictly a **participant** and **Platform Service Capability** in the TANTRA ecosystem. It complies with the BCAB Master Directive by:
* Avoiding orchestration of business processes.
* Decoupling mathematical problem formulation from underlying execution technologies.
* Producing deterministic, replay-safe evidence packets for every execution.

## 2. Boundary Enforcement
All downstream interactions with solvers are routed through strictly governed Adapters. The fabric relies on the upstream TANTRA platform for authentication, authorization, and intent mapping.
